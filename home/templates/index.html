So why should you use DailyTrack?

Mood Tracker

Take your daily tests and keep track of your moods to see if they can disclose more things about you and your thought process.

Modern Format

Bringing the services in an online platofrm will bring comfort to people who prefer doing transactions through devices and offer more privacy to those who need to seek out help.

Accomodating to All Users

The app can be used by general users, patients, and their psychologists. Everyone can take part and have their records kept for chronological keeping!

Easy to Use Application for Everybody

DailyTrack has functionalities that are easy to follow along and are designed to be user friendly. With bright colors and clearly defined features, the application accomodates to the needs of all types of users, whether patients or specialists, and makes sure that they get what they need from the app without much difficulty.

Try it Free

Modern Design with Essential Features

DailyTrack's features mainly revolve around giving patients an avenue to track and take note of their mental health while giving mental health specialists an easier way to manage such heavy bulks of information. Other features that help make the experience an easier venture on both sides are the appointment maker, the timely reports of the moods, and the journals to name some of them.

Try it Free

Questions Approved by Professionals in the Field

The items present across the various sections of the main questionnaire were verified and had heavy involvement from a certified psychologist to ensure the test's credibility and appeal to the potential test-takers.

Try it Free

Developed for both the General Public and Psychologists

Whether you just want to answer the questionnaires to find your mood or seek professional help in relation with your results, DailyTrack has an avenue for you. Mental health specialists can also use the application to keep track of their patient's appointments, current moods, and journal entries without needing tons of paper files that take up a lot of space like before.

Features

Appointment Maker

This feature lets the user make appointments and also allows them to also accept, decline, or edit proposed appointment plans with their mental health specialist.

Stepping Stones

The main feature of the app, housing the 4-part questionnaire that needs to be answered to determine the user's current mood and mental state.

Emoticard

The card that contains a summary of your answers from the questionnaire and what they could mean in translation.
